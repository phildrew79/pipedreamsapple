About the shells and shell sets

Using a well-known "hanky vase" style, these pieces are inspired by sea shells, in particular the giant clams we have seen in the Indian Ocean. The colour schemes generally show clear separation between inside and outside, allowing different presentation from different angles, with a vivid contrasting lip-wrap. To show off the inside, outside and wrap and how they combine, these are usually sold as sets, but individuals are available.
